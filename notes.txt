fractals of thought scatter like glass marbles,  
a clock ticks sideways, measuring echoes instead of time.  
The horizon folds into origami shapes  
dragons, ships, and forgotten letters  
while shadows dance in binary rhythms,  
half dream, half algorithm, all alive.
